Build Timeline

My timeline of events in the build so far. Hover over each panel to read the descriptions. Idea used from Alvaro Trigo's 4-panel timeline example (https://codepen.io/alvarotrigo/pen/GRMKqoX).

Oct 2023

Vehicle Purchase

I purchased the 2017 Audi A6 2.0T in stock form from an auction. This was the starting point for the whole project.

Mar 2025

APR Stage 1

First upgrade. Stage 1 ECU file improved throttle response and power on 91 octane.

Apr 2025

Injen Cold Air Intake

Installed the Injen cold air intake system to improve airflow and turbo sound.

Oct 2025

Downpipe Upgrade

Installed a JHM downpipe to reduce backpressure and support future tunes.

2026+

Next Phase

Intercooler upgrade + Stage 2 from Unitronic.
