About the Build

In October 2023, I purchased this 2017 Audi A6 2.0T from an auction, and began to transform it from a luxury sedan into a tuned machine. The C7.5 platform offers a great foundation, combining Audi's build quality with the EA888.3 turbocharged engine.

My goal behind this build focuses on utilizing the max performance while maintaining the car's daily element. I wanted to create a sleeper vehicle that appears stock but delivers a lot more performance over stock.

I started with the APR Stage 1 tune running a 91 octane file in March 2025. This was followed by supporting modifications including an Injen cold air intake system in April 2025 and a JHM downpipe in October 2025.

Looking ahead, my build will continue to evolve. Future plans include upgrading to an intercooler, Unitronic Stage 2 and bigger turbo, which will push the platform even further.

Build Inspiration

Influences and resources that shaped this build:

  • APR Engineering: Industry-leading ECU calibration and performance software, trusted by enthusiasts worldwide.
  • JHM Performance: Specialist in Audi/VW performance parts, known for their USA built engineering.
  • AudiWorld Forums: Community knowledge base where experienced drivers share results.
  • VW Vortex Community: Cross-platform expertise from the EA888 engine family.
  • Engineering Explained: Educational content that provides a deeper understanding.
